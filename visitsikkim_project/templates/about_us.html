About Monastery360

Digitally preserving and sharing the profound spiritual and cultural heritage of Sikkim's monasteries.

Our Mission

To make Sikkim's unique Himalayan legacy accessible to a global audience, fostering a deeper understanding through immersive technology.

Our Vision

To be the world's most comprehensive digital guide to Sikkim's monastic traditions, ensuring this timeless heritage inspires generations to come.

The Story Behind Monastery360

Monastery360 was born from a deep appreciation for Sikkim's serene landscapes and a desire to protect its invaluable cultural treasures. We realized that while these monasteries are rich in history, their stories, art, and wisdom remain inaccessible to many. Our project aims to solve this by creating a digital bridge, transcending geographical barriers and preserving fragile heritage for the future.
